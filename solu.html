SOLUTION

The main goal of our app is to increase the usage of mHealth apps by targeting users with notifications to launch the app when they are most likely to be engaged. By increasing usage, we believe this will help the apps work more effectively.

Constructs

An application that tracks users and classifys good times to use the apps, such as when the user is not moving at home or at a certain time of day, and launches a notification for the user to use a mHealth app at those times.

Operationalize

Monitor the accelerometer, GPS, and screen state and receive feedback from users to find how to classify these times as either good, bad, or neutral.
